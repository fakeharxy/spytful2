    On your turn

    Click a card in your hand, then a matching hex to Drop In to the board. This will begin your route and place you on the board.

    Add to your route by clicking on cards in your hand. Your route must end on an extract card.

    To block your opponents routes, click a hex segment, then a matching outpost card to build an outpost. Your opponents cannot make routes through your outposts.

    Once you have a valid route (it cannot cross itself, go through water or begin or end on a castle), click on your movement stack to start your route. Click the hexes on the board to draw your route. Once you are finished, click Finish Extraction to claim your points.

    At the end of each turn, draw cards from the pool. You can draw up to 2, but only one from the face-down deck.
