    On your turn

    Click a card, then a matching hex to "drop in". This will begin your route and place you on the board.

    Click a hex segment, then a matching outpost card to make an outpost. Your opponents cannot make routes through your outposts.

    Click on cards in your hand to make your route longer. Your route must end on an extract card.

    Once you have a valid route, click on your movement stack to start your route. Click the hexes on the board to draw your route. Once you are finished, click Finish Extraction to claim your points.

    At the end of each turn, draw cards from the pool. You can draw up to 2, but only one from the face-down deck.
